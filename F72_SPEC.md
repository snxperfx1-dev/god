## PRINCIPLES

**1 ‚Äî Curves own phases.** Not `phase machine ‚Üí curve`, but `curve ‚Üí phase`.
Phases are descriptions, not engines.

**2 ‚Äî Curves spawn curves.** Every reversal / CHoCH / displacement / transition
creates a child curve. Recursive geometry ‚Äî parent ‚Üí child ‚Üí child.

**3 ‚Äî Compression determines recursion depth.** Wide convexity ‚Üí large recursion.
Compressed convexity ‚Üí failure swing ‚Üí tiny recursion. Same geometry, different scale.

**4 ‚Äî New High ‚âÝ Retracement.** After a new high begins a Phase-2 CHoCH ‚Üí inverse
curve ‚Üí recursive cycle ‚Üí further recursions before transition completes. One to
four recursions depending on compression.

**5 ‚Äî Highs and Supply/Demand behave identically.** Same psychology at highs and
HTF supply/demand: recursive curves, internal CHoCHs, induction, liquidation,
entry cycles. The only difference is compression.

**6 ‚Äî Supply and demand are environments, not endpoints.** Inside supply:
curve ‚Üí counter curve ‚Üí induction ‚Üí liquidation ‚Üí terminal curve ‚Üí entry cycle.
Supply, demand and ranges are all alive. Everything is a curve.

**7 ‚Äî Participants create displacement.** Layers: 61.8 (first interference),
70 (ICT crowd), 78.6 (late manipulation), FU Flip (true induction). The flip zone
is the true battlefield ‚Äî fibs are participant reactions; the flip is energy transfer.

**8 ‚Äî Ownership.** Every curve owns price. Ownership belongs to **dominant residual
energy**, not timeframe. It transfers only when the child completes its lifecycle
AND parent energy is exhausted. Otherwise: Camp B ‚Üí merge ‚Üí Camp A.

**9 ‚Äî Merge.** Counter curve touches parent FU. If the parent survives and the child
collapses, ownership returns (B ‚Üí A). No campaign transfer.

**10 ‚Äî Genuine transfer.** Requires terminal exhaustion + parent invalidation +
new energy dominance. Only then Campaign A ‚Üí Campaign B.

**11 ‚Äî Entry cycles** occur inside *terminal induction*, not expansion. Inside
terminal zones: induction ‚Üí liquidation ‚Üí recursive cycle ‚Üí recursive induction ‚Üí
recursive liquidation ‚Üí entry. Compression controls speed.

**12 ‚Äî Curve Stack ‚âÝ timeframes.** Not M1/M5/H1/H4, but curve inside curve inside
curve. Recursion is **event-generated, not timeframe-generated**.

**13 ‚Äî Direction derives from energy:** `sign(close - origin) + velocity + energy + convexity`.
Direction is a consequence, not bullish/bearish.

**14 ‚Äî The phase engine disappears.** There is no `0‚Üí1‚Üí2‚Üí3‚Üí4‚Üí5`. There is
`CurveNode.state`, which emits:

- Point 4 Origin (birth)
- Expansion (energy increasing)
- Expansion Pre-Convexity (convexity appearing)
- Expansion Induction (counter participants emerging)
- Expansion Liquidity (liquidity transfer beginning)
- New High / New Low (energy climax)
- Transition (recursive inversion; may contain 1‚Äì4 child curves)
- Retracement (uniform trend, low displacement)
- Retracement Pre-Convexity (compression forming)
- Retracement Induction (participants countering)
- Retracement Liquidity (terminal liquidation)
- Demand Return / Supply Return (terminal return)

Phases describe state. State belongs to CurveNode.

**15 ‚Äî The Curve Tree** is the final architecture: a `CurveNode` with children[],
parent, energy, residual energy, convexity, compression, displacement, ownership,
maturity, interfaces, phase, flip zone, state. Everything else derives.

**16 ‚Äî Compression Persistence (Force).** After a structure break + pullback, the
question is not "how deep will it retrace?" but **"can the counter side even build
room?"** Tight compression + concentrated residual energy + few recursions ‚áí the
opposite side suffocates, the **extreme becomes irrelevant**, and support/resistance
**migrates to the 0.5‚Äì0.618 band** of the expansion leg (continuation; shallow
pullbacks; no extreme retest). Broad compression + widening convexity + growing
recursion ‚áí energy leaks, the **origin stays in play**, return-to-extreme risk rises
and ownership is about to transfer. It is not momentum ‚Äî it is how much breathing
room the opposite side is being allowed, measured recursively at every scale.

## THE ULTIMATE PRINCIPLE

Everything reduces to one thing: **energy cannot travel in a straight line forever,
therefore it curves.** Wyckoff, ICT, Fibonacci, sessions, supply, demand, expansion,
retracement, induction, liquidation, campaigns, entries, transitions ‚Äî all are
manifestations of one object: the Curve. The final engine is not a phase machine;
it is a living recursive geometry engine built from nested CurveNodes.

**17 ‚Äî Narrative Lineage (continuity).** The hold-vs-abandon judgement is not made by
one curve but by the **sequence** of curves: origin ‚Üí entry ‚Üí progress ‚Üí entry. Each
completed pullback votes **SUPPORT** (shallow retrace + tightening compression),
**DEGRADE** (deep retrace + broadening), or **TRANSFER** (a new story begins). A
**converging** chain (successively shallower retraces, rising compression, more
aggressive participants) ‚áí the story is *strengthening*; a **diverging** chain ‚áí
*weakening*. You weren't trading curves ‚Äî you were trading the **continuity of belief
expressed through curves**, a layer above the tree itself.
